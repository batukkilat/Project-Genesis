# Search 04 — duplicated gates hold steady; evolution rewires the economy around them

Fourth `genesis search` run: three seeds from the committed search-03
artifacts racing gate count in one pool — the confirmed champion
(2 env-gated culls), its rank-4 cousin (3 gates, never confirmed), and
the de-gated branch (0 gates). Spec:
[sweeps/search-04.ron](../../../sweeps/search-04.ron). The question
(search-03's open follow-up): do duplicated environmental gates
*diverge* — different radii / info floors / riders settling into
distinct roles — or stay redundant copies drifting together?

Machine: 4-core cloud box, release build from `9c8ce93` (rustc
1.96.1). 83 individuals over 8 generations in 3 626.5 s, top 3
confirmed at 6 000 ticks. Artifacts in `2026-07-16-search-04/`;
byte-reproducible from the spec. Operationally the run was killed by
the environment twice (container restart on the 2026-07-16 shift
after generation 1; a silent external kill mid-generation-8 on this
one) and completed on the third launch — each relaunch reproduced
every already-committed artifact byte-for-byte before continuing,
which is the spec-purity contract doing disaster recovery for free.

## Outcome in one paragraph

The 2-gate seed's line swept: the entire leaderboard top half descends
from g000-i000, the 3-gate cousin's line faded after early headline
leadership, and the de-gated line went extinct after generation 2. The
gates themselves barely moved for eight generations — and that is the
finding. Specialization did not come as drift in the gates' mutable
knobs; it came as `RewireCondition` ops systematically moving
*information* bounds onto matter and energy in the rules around the
gates, three consecutive fitness leaps in three generations, with the
same rewire discovered independently three times. The screen podium
then detonated in confirmation — **both** the screen #1 (g008-i005,
99.50) and #2 (g008-i001, 84.29) capped past 600 k bonds at 6 k ticks —
leaving **g008-i003 the honest champion: confirmed fitness 94.80 /
headline 566.0 at 6 k, uncapped, on 28 520 bonds** — +6.6 % over
search-03's confirmed champion with an eighth of its bond mass.

## Findings

### 1. Gate count: two beats three beats zero (the race the spec set up)

The champion lineage descends purely from the 2-gate seed:
g000-i000 → g002-i004 → g003-i006 → g004-i000 → g005-i007 →
g006-i000 → g007-i009 → g008-i003. The 3-gate cousin seeded the
pool's highest single headline (g001-i007, 339.7) and parented
generations 2–3, but no descendant of it survived selection past
generation 3; the de-gated seed's best child parented generation 2 and
its line then vanished. Truncation selection got three chances to
prefer more or fewer gates and settled on two every time. One
late-generation probe agrees from the other side: g008-i009 *dropped*
gate r7 and finished rank 5 — viable, never leading — the same shape
as search-03's de-gated branch.

### 2. The gates froze…

Along the entire champion lineage the two env-gated culls kept the
seed's radii (3.156 / 2.201), the frozen climate window ([0.6, 1.0] —
mutation v1 cannot touch env_cond), the culled info band (≤ 0.45), and
the probability (0.05) bit-for-bit. The only gate mutation that
survived selection on the winning line in eight generations was one
rider doubling (g002-i004: r1's matter rider 0.00077 → 0.00138). The
divergence the spec imagined — copies drifting apart in radius or
floor — did not happen: those knob settings were already load-bearing.

### 3. …and evolution rewired the economy around them

The three final fitness leaps on the champion line all came from
`RewireCondition` (the operator that moves a bounds interval from one
quantity to another), and every surviving rewire points the same way —
*away* from information conditions:

- g006-i000 (+2.0): rewire r9.other_cond information→matter (r9 is a
  g005 duplicate of the IMPRINT rule).
- g007-i009 (+1.3): rewire r0.other_cond information→energy — the
  *uniform* cull stops selecting on information, keying on energy
  poverty instead. Bond count collapses 144 k → 20 k while fitness
  rises; the information term, not structure mass, carries the gain.
- g008-i003 (+2.5, the champion): two information *riders* (0.0021 /
  0.0024 per event) threaded onto existing rules — the search-03
  finding 3 mechanism, reproduced under a different lineage.

Convergence seals it: `rewire r5.other_cond information→matter` (BIND
stops requiring an informed partner) was discovered independently by
g008-i005 and g008-i006 from *different parents*, and 28 of the
leaderboard's op chains carry a rewire. Under a fitness that rewards
structure-held information, evolution keeps *removing* information
preconditions — plausibly because an information condition restricts
which particles may act, while moving the same band to energy/matter
widens participation and lets the imprint economy concentrate
information faster. The gates specialized along the one categorical
axis mutation exposes — the selected quantity — not the numeric knobs
the spec enumerated.

### 4. Confirmation carnage: the screen podium was two time bombs

g008-i005 (screen 99.50 — a +27 % leap) and g008-i001 (84.29) both
capped past 600 k bonds in confirmation. i005 is an information
detonator: its BIND rewire lets bonds form without informed partners,
and its *screen* information total is already 2.1 × 10¹¹ — the
saturating fitness (triple-ln on information) is precisely why that
detonation still screened at only 99.5, fitness v1 damping a regime
the raw scalar would have crowned. After two searches where the
screen #1 detonated, this one killed #1 *and* #2: `confirm_top 3` —
adopted for exactly this risk — is now the empirical floor for this
regime family, and the pattern has a name: the screen horizon selects
regimes scheduled to explode just past it.

### 5. The champion economy: leaner again

g008-i003 confirms at 94.80 (vs search-03's 88.90, +6.6 %) with
28 520 bonds at 6 k where its grandparent regime held 225 403 — an
eighth of the bond mass — and 360 k structure-held information (the
2026-07-15 correction's affordability worry dissolves for this line:
bonds *shrink* along it). Same direction as search-03 finding 3: on
fitness v1 the winning move is more information on less structure,
the opposite of the condensation the raw headline scalar prices
(2026-07-15 anatomy). The 20 k corpus-horizon evaluation — affordable
at last for this family (212 s; bonds stay bounded, 28.5 k at 6 k →
58.3 k at 20 k) — makes the trade-off exact:
`g008-i003.20k.score.ron` records headline **2 192.4**, *below* the
search-02 champion's 2 421.6 and the condensing packs (bands 2 712,
actual 3 631), while its search fitness at 20 k is 106.97 — the
highest fitness this project has measured at any horizon. Fitness and
the raw exit scalar now disagree about which discovered regime is
best, on measured numbers: the exact tension Q-2026-07-15-A parks for
the owner.

## Status of the follow-up question

The concrete case search-04 was designed to detect — gate copies
specializing against the frozen env_cond window — did not
materialize: the copies stayed near-identical while selection
restructured the rules around them, and the strongest gate-touching
mutations were a rewire (detonated) and a drop (rank 5). The case for
an env-gate mutation operator is therefore *weaker*, not stronger:
mutation found ample fitness elsewhere, and nothing pressed against
the frozen climate window. Parked observations for future runs: (a)
the screen keeps selecting delayed detonators — a mid-screen
bond-growth-rate mark was the obvious cheap instrument, and it was
**measured and rejected the same night** (Q-2026-07-17-A): at the 3 k
screen the detonators' final-third bond growth (1.18 / 1.35) is
indistinguishable from the honest champion's (1.17) — the detonation
is scheduled wholly past the horizon and leaves no signature the
screen can see, so `bonds_growth_late` ships in every record as
report-only observability and no flag pretends to predict; (b)
whether the de-informationalizing rewire direction persists under a
fitness that samples information at the confirm horizon rather than
the screen.
