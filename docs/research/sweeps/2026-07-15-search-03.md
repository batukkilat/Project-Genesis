# Search 03 — evolution keeps the environmental gate, and duplicates it

Third `genesis search` run: the flat-world sieve and the cline-world
gradient-sieve competing in one pool, instrument settings carried from
search-02 (σ 0.6 × 3 ops/child, 200 k/600 k caps). Spec:
[sweeps/search-03.ron](../../../sweeps/search-03.ron). The question
(the open half of the selection-pressure deliverable): do descendants
keep the env-gated cull and exploit the gradient, or strip it and revert
to flat-world dynamics?

Machine: 4-core Intel Xeon @ 2.80 GHz (cloud box), release build
`ddc0db8` line (rustc 1.96.1). 62 individuals over 6 generations in
2 866.6 s, top 2 confirmed at 6 000 ticks. Artifacts in
`2026-07-15-search-03/`; byte-reproducible from the spec.

## Outcome in one paragraph

The gradient-sieve lineage swept the pool: ranks 1–26 all descend from
the cline-world seed (itself rank 27), while the flat sieve seed sits at
rank 38, its best children at ranks 30–31, its line extinct by
generation 2. The winning lineage did not merely tolerate the
environmental gate — **generation 3 duplicated the env-gated cull**
(`dup r1→r7` in g003-i003), and every top-10 individual carries 2–3
env-gated culls at overlapping radii where the seed had one. A parallel
branch that stripped every gate (g004-i008 → g005-i009, ranks 11 and 6)
survived but never took the lead. The screen champion g006-i009 (79.17,
+4.0 % over its seed) **detonated in confirmation** — capped past 600 k
bonds at 6 k ticks — so the honest all-time champion is its parent
**g005-i005: confirmed fitness 88.90 / headline 751.7 at 6 k, uncapped**,
a regime holding +50 % structure-held information at *half* the seed's
bond count.

## Findings

### 1. The environmental gate earned its keep (the spec's question, answered)

Mutation applies uniform DropRule pressure — any rule can be deleted any
generation, and drops were common everywhere in the ancestry. Under that
pressure the env-gated cull was not only retained along the winning line
but *multiplied*: the confirmed champion carries two env-gated absorbs
(radii 3.16 and 2.20, both `field 0 ≥ 0.6`, info floor 0.45), and two
rank-3/4 cousins carry three. Doubling the gated rule doubles the
per-encounter cull rate exactly where the field is high — evolution
turned the selection dial the pack authored, in the direction of *more*
spatial differential. Meanwhile the de-gated branch (ranks 6, 11)
proves stripping was reachable and viable — it just lost: gated
descendants hold 9.8–12.5 k structure-held information where the
de-gated branch holds 4.8–7.7 k. On fitness v1, geography pays.

### 2. The flat world was displaced, not out-evolved

Sieve's lineage never got worse — its children sit within ±0.4 % of
their seed — it simply plateaued (the search-01 lesson again, now with
compound steps) while the cline world's children found +1.3 % by
generation 3 and +4.0 % by generation 6. With truncation selection over
the whole pool, a plateau is extinction: no sieve descendant was bred
after generation 1. The controlled part of this comparison is weaker
than search-02's (different seeds occupy different neighborhoods), but
the direction is consistent with finding 1: the gradient world is
better ground for this fitness, not merely equal.

### 3. The champion economy: more information on half the bonds

The winning lineage's fitness came from the information term, not the
structure count: seed → champion moves structure-held information
7 287 → 10 970 (+50 %) and bonds 154 k → 81 k (−48 %), with structures
roughly flat (837 → 922). The ancestry shows repeated tiny
`transfer.information` riders added to existing rules (0 → ~0.001 per
event) — mutation kept threading information flow through rules that
formerly moved only matter/energy. Cheaper structure carrying more
signal is exactly what fitness v1 was designed to reward; it is also
the opposite of the condensation direction the headline scalar prices
(the 2026-07-15 anatomy result) — this search *reduced* bond mass while
climbing fitness.

### 4. Confirmation flipped the podium again

Screen rank 1 (g006-i009) detonated 900–3 000 ticks past the screen
horizon (capped > 600 k bonds at 6 k; its screen bond count was 62 k) —
the same shape as search-02's g006-i000. Its parent g005-i005, 1.3 %
behind at the screen, confirmed clean. Two searches in a row, the
screen's #1 was a time bomb and the #2 was the real champion:
`confirm_top ≥ 2` is now empirically the minimum, and any claim about a
"champion" must cite the confirmation record, not the screen
(precedent: search-02 finding 4, now twice observed).

### 5. Determinism, cross-context, again

The gradient-sieve seed's screen record inside the search
(state hash `0x94c751d2454715cc`) is bit-identical to the standalone
`genesis score` record committed hours earlier by a different code path
(`2026-07-15-gradient-sieve/gradient-sieve-3k.score.ron`) — the search
evaluates through the same `score_run` path and the same universe falls
out, which is the one-representation rule doing its job in the tooling.

## Status of the selection-pressure deliverable

Both halves are now answered: the sieve (2026-07-13) showed the rule
vocabulary expresses information-gated survival; gradient-sieve
(2026-07-15) showed the environment can set that pressure spatially with
one env gate; and this search shows **searched variants exploit the
coupling rather than discarding it** — qualitatively new dynamics in the
sense the deliverable asked (an evolved regime that doubles its
environmental differentiation and re-routes information through its
economy). Open follow-ups worth a future run: whether the duplicated
gates *diverge* (different radii/floors specializing into distinct
climate bands) under longer searches, and whether any of this regime
family confirms honestly at 20 k (none was evaluated there — the
champion's 6 k bond count, 81 k... at seed-like growth rates a 20 k run
is affordable and would slot it into the corpus table).
