# 2026-07-20 — search-05: screening at the detonation horizon

**Question.** Three consecutive searches (02, 03, 04) ended with the 3 k
screen's #1 — search-04: #1 *and* #2 — detonating in confirmation, and
the cheap mid-screen growth mark was measured and rejected the same
night it was proposed (Q-2026-07-17-A): the detonation leaves no 3 k
signature to flag. Search-05 applies the one remaining lever that needs
no new instrument: **screen where the detonation fires.** Screens run
6 000 ticks — the horizon at which every observed detonator has already
capped — with the 6 k-scale bond cap (600 k) as the screen's own
breaker, and confirmation doubles the horizon again (12 k, cap 1.2 M).
The pool seeds search-04's honest champion (g008-i003) against both of
its detonators (g008-i005, g008-i001), so the horizon hypothesis is
measured at generation 0, in-pool. Spec: `sweeps/search-05.ron`
(seed 20260720, 6 generations × 10, σ 0.6 × 3 ops, confirm_top 3).

Machine: 4-core cloud box, release build `842da05` (rustc 1.96.1);
63 evaluations + 3 confirmations in 7 076.6 s.

## Finding 1 — the 6 k screen sees through the delayed detonators

At generation 0 both detonator seeds tripped the breaker mid-screen:
g008-i005 (screen fitness 99.5 at 3 k in search-04, the highest ever
recorded at that horizon) capped at tick 5 600 with 1.107 M bonds and
an information total of 1.9 × 10²⁵; g008-i001 capped at tick 5 200
with 955 k bonds. Both took fitness 0 and were culled at the first
selection. The honest champion's 6 k screen reproduced its committed
search-04 confirmation **bit-for-bit** — 28 520 bonds, 360 k
information, fitness 94.80 — across builds and searches, the
determinism contract doing exactly what it promises.

Mutation then *re-discovered* detonation six times (three children in
g001, one each in g002–g004; fastest: 692 k bonds by tick 2 100), and
the screen killed every one in the generation it appeared. The final
two generations produced twenty honest children and not a single cap.

## Finding 2 — the detonation streak is broken: all three confirmations held

For the first time in the program's history the screen's #1 survived
confirmation. All-time top three by screen fitness — g005-i003
(139.05), g006-i001 (138.75), g006-i005 (138.66) — ran the full 12 k
uncapped with bounded bond growth (36 k / 28 k / 36 k bonds at 12 k)
and **bounded headline equal to raw** in all three records: nothing
condensed, nothing detonated one horizon out. The confirmed champion
g005-i003 scores 970.22 at 12 k (fitness 152.61). Three searches
crowned delayed detonators; moving the screen past the detonation
horizon ended the pattern at a cost of ~2× screen wall-clock — cheaper
than the confirmations it un-wastes.

One caution survives the win: g004-i004 screened *honest* at exactly
482 k bonds — under the cap, condensing in plain sight. A regime
scheduled to blow at 8 k would pass this screen as the 3 k detonators
passed theirs. The horizon race is mitigated, not won; the 2×-horizon
confirmation stays the backstop.

## Finding 3 — question (b) answered: the rewire direction reverses

Search-04's signature move — `RewireCondition` deleting *information*
preconditions (found independently three times, 28 rewires on its
final leaderboard, all one direction) — does **not** persist when
fitness samples at 6 k. The search-05 leaderboard's rewires point both
ways, including the exact opposite (`matter→information` at ranks 4, 6,
20; `energy→information` at 14, 16). The search-04 direction was a
horizon artifact: at 3 k the imprint economy's compounding has barely
begun, so widening participation (dropping information gates) is worth
more than holding information. At 6 k the compounding dominates
everything else in the fitness.

## Finding 4 — the fitness gain is (almost) entirely the information term, and the term is saturating

Screen fitness rose 94.80 → 139.05 (+47 % at the same horizon, same
family). Decomposing fitness v1: the champion's information total at
6 k is 5.7 × 10³⁰ against the seed's 3.6 × 10⁵, and the info factor
`1+ln(1+ln(1+I))` moves 3.62 → 5.27 — a ratio of 1.46, i.e. the entire
fitness leap to within rounding. The structure and lifetime terms
barely moved. What evolution actually optimized this run was
information mass, and it drove it to the **clamp**: the confirmed
champion's largest structure holds 3.8 × 10³¹ across 50 members —
mean 7.6 × 10²⁹ per particle, within 25 % of `information_max`
(10³⁰, Q-2026-07-06-B). These are imprint-web regimes whose particles
saturate their information capacity; past that point the info term is
measuring the clamp, not a signal (ln ln compresses 10³⁰ → 10³³ into
~4 % of fitness), so at 6 k+ horizons fitness v1's information term
has effectively stopped discriminating between information-rich
regimes. Recorded as a known instrument limit for whenever the
fitness question is next opened — no change proposed here (the term
still separates these regimes cleanly from information-poor ones, and
the raw exit scalar is unaffected).

## Corpus context

The confirmed champion's 20 k evaluation
(`g005-i003.20k.score.ron`): raw headline **1 699.3**, bounded
**equal to raw** — the regime stays honest at every measured horizon
(51 429 bonds at 20 k, largest structure 142 members, information
total 2.6 × 10³²). On the 20 k corpus leaderboard that places it
*below* both earlier discovered champions on the raw scalar
(g006-i009 2 421.6, g008-i003 2 192.4) and fifth on the bounded one
(sandbox 2 178.5 > g006-i009 2 106.6 > g008-i003 1 894.2 >
full-stack 1 806.3 > g005-i003 1 699.3), while carrying the highest
fitness the program has ever measured — 159.16 at 20 k against
g008-i003's 106.97 (+49 %). The divergence Q-2026-07-15-A parks is
now three searches wide and monotone: each successive champion is
*fitter* and scores *lower* on the raw exit scalar — fitness v1
keeps walking away from what the exit criterion measures, in the
direction the phase's own philosophy calls honest.

## Status of the open questions

(a) **Closed for the observed regime family**: screening at the
horizon where detonation fires removes the screen/confirm mismatch —
at double wall-clock per eval, with the slow-condenser caveat of
finding 2. (b) **Closed**: de-informationalization was a 3 k artifact;
the 6 k-sampled fitness rewards the opposite and saturates (finding
4). New parked observation: if a future search wants fitness to keep
discriminating at long horizons, the information term needs a
saturation-aware form — an instrument fork for a future spec, not
this one.
